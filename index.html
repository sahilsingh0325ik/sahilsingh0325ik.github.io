The History of Basketball

What is Basketball?

Basketball is a popular team sport where two teams of five players each compete on a rectangular court to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop, while preventing the opposing team from doing the same with the objective being to outscore the opposition.

There are 3 main ways to score

1. Free Throws

2. Field Goals

3. Three Pointers

The Facts

The game was created by James Naismith, a physical education teacher, on December 1, 1891 at what is now Springfield College.

The creation of the game was met with great excitement and became a collegiate sport within a decade with the first professional league being established in 1898.

Basketball became incorporated in the Olympic Games in 1936 and increasingly grew in popularity after World War 2.
